The Mathematics competition of 2023 has been launched, and the deadline to register is 20th of October, 2023

OUR HISTORY

This competition is hosting its 5th international session this year, with winners over the years being from Cameroon, Nigeria, Ghana, HongKong and Zambia. The global space of this competition makes it more competitive and entertaining for the competitors.

OUR PURPOSE

The competition seeks to challenge your ability to think critically in the field of Mathematics, how you can analyze and approach complex and indirect mathematics problems, how you can think creatively and concisely in the environment of mathematics.

COMPETITION SCOPE

The competition being international assesses the competitors in the fields of:

  • Geometry
  • Number Theory
  • Calculus
  • Functions

These topics will cover a wider scope of other sub topics like Series and Sequences, Binomial Expansion, and others.

HOW IS THE COMPETITION ORGANIZED

The competition holds in two stages,

The first stage is an MCQ(MULTIPLE CHOICE QUESTIONS) which is taken online, from home. The first stage tests for the competitor's Aptitude, Accuracy and Critical Thinking. It holds for 2hrs, and covers 40% of the competition.

The second stage is Structural and lasts for 3 days. This covers 60% of the competition. How solutions will be submitted will be communicated to those who are registered.

Before these two stages, a Qualification Challenge will be administered to the registered students, on the first week of November, to select those who will be eligible for the First and Second stages, which will hold on the 27th to the 30th of December, 2023. The Qualification session is a series of 20 Basic MCQ questions which applicants will answer within 40 mins. Your score in the Qualification round doesn't count, but having a 12/20 makes you a good fit for the competition Stage. This Qualification round is meant to give applicants an overview and insights of what the maths@njahmi End of Year competition (MEOYC) looks like, and to inform them how ready they are.
